Bubble Burst!


Bubble Burst is the brainchild of Genevieve Conty.

After the 2016 Preseidential election she realized that she was living in a bubble,insulated and surrounded by like-minded people.

The goal of BubbleBurst is to give the user a convenient way to hear what thought leaders from all ends of the political spectrum are saying.

The idea is to foster true listening, discourse, and critical thinking while venturing outside of each of our individual comfort zones.
